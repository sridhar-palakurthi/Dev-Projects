Navigate to the Facebook Developers website(https://developers.facebook.com/)

sign-in with your Facebook account credentials.

click Apps > Add a New App, Enter App Name in Display Name.

Choose your Category.

Now, Facebook will create AppId and Secret. Copy them.

On the Left hand pane, 

Click Settings> Choose platform as Web > then Enter your site URL:http://localhost:8080/

Click on Save changes.

Now in the Source paste the AppId and Secret Key wherever mentioned
